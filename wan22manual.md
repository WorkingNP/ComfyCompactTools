# Wan2.2 TI2V (5B) Recommended Settings (Quick Manual)

## Recommended defaults (official template)
- Resolution: 1280 x 704 (landscape)
- Frames (length): 121
- FPS: 24
- Steps: 20
- CFG: 5.0
- Sampler: uni_pc
- Scheduler: simple
- Seed: -1 (random)

## Prompt presets (defaults in manifest)
- Positive: "masterpiece, best quality, high detail"
- Negative: "worst quality, low quality, bad anatomy, bad hands, extra fingers, missing fingers, deformed, disfigured, blurry, watermark, text, jpeg artifacts"

## Notes
- Keep start_image resolution matched to width/height above.
- If you want a faster preview, reduce length/steps.
